Baked Salmon

Description

This baked salmon recipe is a quick and easy dish featuring salmon fillets cooked in a creamy tomato sauce. The preparation takes only 10 minutes, and the oven does the rest, leaving you free to cook noodles or rice as a side. The sauce combines ingredients like cream, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and fresh herbs such as oregano, thyme, and parsley, creating a rich and flavorful base. Cherry tomatoes add a fresh touch to the dish.

The salmon can be baked directly from frozen (with a slightly longer cooking time) or thawed beforehand. Simply place the salmon in a baking dish, cover it with the prepared sauce, and bake at 160째C (320째F) for approximately 25 minutes. Serve it with pasta or rice for a complete meal that's perfect for families, couples, or singles looking for a simple yet delicious dinner.
